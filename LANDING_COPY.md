## FAQ

**Q1: Is this actually free? What is the catch?**  
Yes, the audit is free. The catch is that it is also a lead-gen tool for Credex, so if your savings are meaningful you may see an invitation to talk about discounted AI credits.

**Q2: Do you store my spending data?**  
Yes. The app stores audit data in Supabase so results can be rendered, shared, and optionally emailed back to you. If you submit your email, that gets stored with the audit too.

**Q3: How accurate are the savings estimates?**  
They are directional, not accounting-grade. The estimates are based on public pricing pages, the values you enter, and deterministic rules about plan fit, overpay, and alternatives.

**Q4: What is Credex and why did they build this?**  
Credex sells discounted AI credits sourced from companies that overforecast their usage. They built SpendLens because teams often know they are spending a lot on AI, but do not know where the waste is or whether credits could help.

**Q5: We already negotiated enterprise pricing — is this tool still useful for us?**  
Usually yes, but for a different reason. If you already have custom pricing, the tool is less useful for exact savings math and more useful for spotting duplicate tools, underused seats, and obvious plan mismatch across the stack.
